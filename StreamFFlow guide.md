# **StreamFFlow – Video Streaming App**

This **Help Center** provides solutions for common account and application issues.

## Fix Login Error

If you see the **Incorrect Password** message, try these steps:

1. Re-enter your password carefully.
2. Make sure **Caps Lock** is turned off.
3. Tap **Forgot Password** if you can’t remember your password.

## Reset Your Password

1. Tap **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter your new password.
3. Re-enter the new password.
4. Tap **Save**.

## Cancel Subscription

1. Log in to **StreamFFlow**. 
2. Go to **Dashboard**.
3. Tap **Subscription**
4. Scroll down to last.
5. Tap **Cancel subscription**.

## Fix App Crashing Issue

If the app keeps crashing, try the following steps:

Steps
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Tap **Apps**.
3. Select **StreamFFlow**.
4. Tap **Force Stop**.
5. Re-open **StreamFFlow**.

>If issue continues then:
• Restart your mobile
• Reinstall **StreamFFlow**.
